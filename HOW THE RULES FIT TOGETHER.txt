The rule system should have three levels
Level 1: Brand rules

Stored in:
DESIGN.md
PRODUCT.md

These define the brand and product.

Level 2: Component rules

Stored in:
design-system/component-rules/
These define each Lego block.

Level 3: Combination rules

Stored in:
design-system/composition-rules.json


Also the staging matters because v1 will teach you what v2 and v3 need to enforce.
