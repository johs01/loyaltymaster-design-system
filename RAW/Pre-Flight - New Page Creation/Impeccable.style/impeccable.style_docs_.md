# Commands

23 commands for design fluency. One skill, six categories, from shaping a new feature to hardening it for production.

## Create

Build something new, from a blank page to a working feature.

[**/impeccable craft** \\
Shape the design, then build it, all in one flow.](https://impeccable.style/docs/craft) [**/impeccable impeccable** \\
The design intelligence behind every command.](https://impeccable.style/docs/impeccable) [**/impeccable shape** \\
Think before you build. Produce a design brief through discovery, not guesswork.](https://impeccable.style/docs/shape)

## Evaluate

Review what you have. Score it, critique it, find what to fix.

[**/impeccable audit** \\
Five-dimension technical quality check with P0 to P3 severity.](https://impeccable.style/docs/audit) [**/impeccable critique** \\
A design review with scoring, persona tests, and automated detection.](https://impeccable.style/docs/critique)

## Refine

Improve one dimension at a time: type, layout, color, motion.

[**/impeccable animate** \\
Purposeful motion that conveys state, not decoration.](https://impeccable.style/docs/animate) [**/impeccable bolder** \\
Push safe designs toward impact without sliding into chaos.](https://impeccable.style/docs/bolder) [**/impeccable colorize** \\
Add strategic color to monochrome interfaces without going garish.](https://impeccable.style/docs/colorize) [**/impeccable delight** \\
Small moments of personality that turn functional into memorable.](https://impeccable.style/docs/delight) [**/impeccable layout** \\
Fix layout, spacing, and visual rhythm.](https://impeccable.style/docs/layout) [**/impeccable overdrive** \\
Push an interface past conventional limits. Shaders, physics, 60fps, cinematic transitions.](https://impeccable.style/docs/overdrive) [**/impeccable quieter** \\
Tone down designs that are shouting without losing their intent.](https://impeccable.style/docs/quieter) [**/impeccable typeset** \\
Fix typography that feels generic, inconsistent, or accidental.](https://impeccable.style/docs/typeset)

## Simplify

Strip complexity. Remove what does not earn its place.

[**/impeccable adapt** \\
Make designs work across screens, devices, and contexts without amputating features.](https://impeccable.style/docs/adapt) [**/impeccable clarify** \\
Rewrite confusing UX copy so interfaces explain themselves.](https://impeccable.style/docs/clarify) [**/impeccable distill** \\
Ruthless subtraction. Strip designs to their essence.](https://impeccable.style/docs/distill)

## Harden

Make it production-ready. Edge cases, performance, polish.

[**/impeccable harden** \\
Make interfaces production-ready. Edge cases, i18n, error states, overflow.](https://impeccable.style/docs/harden) [**/impeccable onboard** \\
Design first-run experiences, empty states, and paths to value.](https://impeccable.style/docs/onboard) [**/impeccable optimize** \\
Diagnose and fix UI performance from LCP to bundle size.](https://impeccable.style/docs/optimize) [**/impeccable polish** \\
The meticulous final pass between good and great.](https://impeccable.style/docs/polish)

## System

Setup and tooling. Design system work, extraction, organization.

[**/impeccable document** \\
Generate a spec-compliant DESIGN.md that captures your visual system so every AI agent stays on-brand.](https://impeccable.style/docs/document) [**/impeccable extract** \\
Pull reusable components, tokens, and patterns into the design system.](https://impeccable.style/docs/extract) [**/impeccable live** \\
Iterate on UI in the browser. Pick an element, drop a comment, get three variants. Accept one and it writes to source.](https://impeccable.style/docs/live) [**/impeccable teach** \\
Teach Impeccable who your product is for, once per project.](https://impeccable.style/docs/teach)
