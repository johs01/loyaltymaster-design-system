# Filled Brief: Brochure Content Body

Source template: `templates/brochure-content.md`

## Artifact Type

Digital brochure or sales content body for Loyaltymaster.

## Shell Scope

Body-only by default. A standalone brochure shell must be explicitly requested
before navbar/header/footer components are allowed.

## Goal

Create a sales content body that explains the offer, shows a use case, provides
proof, keeps pricing inside the approved pricing component, and captures
qualified interest through an approved form.

## Audience

Business owners and sales-qualified prospects evaluating whether sendPUSH fits
their repeat-visit campaign.

## Primary Offer

Start free in 2 minutes and use sendPUSH to create a practical customer-return
campaign.
