# Filled Brief: Landing Page Body

Source template: `templates/landing-page.md`

## Artifact Type

Landing page body for the existing Loyaltymaster production website.

## Shell Scope

Body-only. The existing production website supplies the global chrome.

## Goal

Create a trial-conversion landing page body that explains why sendPUSH helps
repeat-business companies reach customers directly and move visitors toward one
email capture action.

## Audience

Business owners and operators who already depend on returning customers and
need a direct loyalty message channel.

## Primary Action

Start free in 2 minutes by entering an email address.
